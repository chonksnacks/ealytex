About Ealytex

A Sourcing Partner Built for Modern Brands

Ealytex started in 2019 in a 39th Street office with one conviction: brands were wasting too much time sourcing and not enough building product. Six years later, we run full programs for some of retail's biggest names — and a handful of brands no one's heard of yet. Same process either way.

We're not a factory. Not a broker. We're the team that finds the right manufacturing partner for your specific program, builds the sample, manages QC from in-line to pre-shipment, and gets goods to your warehouse. The brands we work with stop thinking about sourcing. That's the point.

Category depth in socks, activewear, seamless, loungewear, sleepwear, and outerwear. 200+ vetted partners across 18 countries. New York, where the market actually lives.

Why Ealytex

The Sourcing Edge You Need

"Six years of building relationships with the manufacturers that take most companies a decade to find."

01
New York Market Intelligence
39th Street doesn't look like much from the outside. But the Garment District is where buyers eat lunch, where trend directors go sample shopping, and where the real production conversations happen. We're in it every day. That kind of proximity isn't something you replicate on a Zoom call.
02
A Curated Network, Not a Directory
There are sourcing databases with thousands of factory listings. We don't use them. Our 200+ partners are manufacturers we've visited, audited, and in some cases had difficult conversations with. That's the filtering process. You get six years of it working for you.
03
Development-First Sourcing
The most expensive mistake in apparel is a bad first bulk run. We've watched it sink good brands. So we don't skip the brief. Fabrics sourced. Fits corrected. Specs locked. Then we produce. That order isn't optional.
04
MOQ Flexibility Across All Tiers
We work with brands doing 500 units and brands doing five million. Same process. Same standard. The number on the purchase order is the only thing that changes.
05
Retailer-Ready from Day One
Walmart's vendor compliance manual is over 40 pages. Target's isn't shorter. We know these requirements cold, and our partners come pre-qualified. Your goods arrive retail-ready. You never have to open an audit email.
06
One Relationship. Full Accountability.
When a production program goes sideways — and something always does somewhere — the last thing you want is three vendors pointing at each other. One team owns everything here. Development, factory, QC, compliance, delivery. One call. One answer.
