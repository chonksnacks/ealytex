About Ealytex

A Manufacturing Partner Built for Modern Brands

Ealytex Global Inc. is a New York–headquartered apparel manufacturing partner supporting brands and retailers across development, sourcing, production, compliance, and delivery. Founded in 2019, Ealytex was built to connect global manufacturing scale with the speed, quality, and accountability modern brands require.

We are an approved and active supplier to leading retailers and brands including Walmart, Costco, PVH, Columbia Sportswear, Amazon, and Target — with production facilities spanning Cambodia, China, Kenya, and Egypt.

U.S.-based leadership. Global factory network. Retailer-ready compliance. From first sample to final delivery.
