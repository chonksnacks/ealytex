Our Story

The Partner Behind the Brand

Founded in 2019, Ealytex Global Inc. is a U.S.-based fashion manufacturer headquartered in New York City. We operate as a vertically integrated partner — fabric sourcing, cut-sew, and logistics under one ownership group — supporting brands from concept through global delivery.

We are an approved and active supplier to some of the world's most recognized retailers and brands, including Walmart, Costco, PVH, Columbia Sportswear, Amazon, and Target.

With 5,000+ employees across our Cambodia facilities, a proprietary fabric mill in China, and offices spanning New York, Shanghai, Suzhou, Tokyo, and beyond — we bring genuine global scale to every partnership.
